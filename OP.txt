[size=6]Auto-shutdown[/size]
This is a server-only mod used for automatic shutting down after some time with no players online. It does "/save-all" 1 minute before expected shutting down.

Sample config file:
[code]general {
   enabled=true
   "force save"=true
   minutes=10
}[/code]
